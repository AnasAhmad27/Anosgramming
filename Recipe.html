How To Make A Delicious Cake

Ingredients:-

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Instructions:-

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x9 inch baking pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add milk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until well combined.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. Slice and serve. Enjoy your delicious cake!
